Applications of Supercapacitors

IoT Devices

Instant power backup for sensors, RTC modules, and stable data communication.

Smart Meters

Supports energy harvesting and retains data during power fluctuations.

Industrial Equipment

Backup power during outages, ensuring continuous operation of systems.

Medical Devices

Short-term emergency power for critical medical applications.

Automotive

Used in start-stop systems and for stabilizing onboard power.

Consumer Electronics

Provides clock and memory backup in everyday electronics.

Renewable Energy

Supports storage and power smoothing in solar and wind systems.
